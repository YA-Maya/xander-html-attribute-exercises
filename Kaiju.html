About the Cafe

Kaiju (Citation: https://www.wordsense.eu/kaiju/) is a giant monster, particularly common in Japanese science fiction films, like Godzilla or Gamera.

Kaiju Cafe was created in 1999 as a half-hatched plan that Erika Lee came up with while awaiting Y2K. The restaurant originally offered vegetarian and vegan food, but many disappointed visitors expected meat to be on the menu. Therefore, burgers made it on the menu, in addition to big salads and Erika’s famous chocolate cake.
